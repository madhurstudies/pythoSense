Know exactly what your
plant is feeling

PythoSense reads soil temperature, humidity, and ambient light intensity from your plant using DHT11/DHT22 and LDR sensors — in real-time.

⚙️
DHT11 / DHT22 Sensors

The core of PythoSense relies on precision digital temperature and humidity sensors. The DHT series provides highly reliable, calibrated digital signal output.

  • 🌡️ Soil Temperature Maintains awareness of root comfort levels.
  • 💧 Humidity Tracking Ensures the microclimate around the plant is ideal.
💡
LDR Light Sensor

A Light Dependent Resistor (LDR) continuously monitors the ambient light intensity hitting the plant's leaves, crucial for photosynthesis.

  • ☀️ Photoperiod Tracking Detects daytime vs nighttime cycles.
  • 🔆 Intensity Measurement Warns if the plant is in overly shaded or harsh light.
📊
Advanced Diagnostics

Beyond raw sensor data, PythoSense calculates derived metrics using agricultural formulas to give you actionable insights.

  • 🌫️ Evaporation Index Calculated via Penman-Monteith (ET₀) approximations based on temp and humidity.
  • 🚿 Water Requirement Combines expected evaporation with current soil moisture to determine daily watering volume.
  • 🪱 Soil Moisture % Direct capacitive measurement of the soil's dielectric constant.
❤️
Plant Health Score

All data streams are fed into an intelligent weighing algorithm that produces a unified 0-100 Health Score. This makes monitoring your plant's status as simple as reading a single grade. We compare current real-time metrics against ideal thresholds for standard houseplants to determine whether conditions are optimal, acceptable, or harmful.
